Frequently Asked Questions

What access do I have on the free plan?

The access you have on a free plan varies depending on the product or service you are using. Generally, free plans offer limited access to features and resources compared to paid plans. For example, in a cloud storage service, the free plan may provide a limited amount of storage space, while the paid plan offers more space and additional features such as file sharing and collaboration. It's best to check the specific details of the free plan you're interested in to understand what access and limitations come with it.

What access do I have on a free trial?

The access you have on a free trial depends on the product or service being offered by the provider. Generally, free trials allow users to try out the features and capabilities of the product or service for a limited time period, which is usually between 7 to 30 days. During the trial period, users can access most, if not all, of the features and functionalities of the product or service. However, there may be some limitations or restrictions on certain features, and the amount of data or usage may also be limited. It's important to read the terms and conditions of the free trial carefully to understand what is included and what limitations may apply.

Does the price go up as my team gets larger?

It depends on the pricing structure of the product or service in question. Some services may charge a flat rate per user regardless of team size, while others may have tiered pricing that increases as the team grows. It's best to check with the specific product or service provider for details on their pricing structure.

How can I cancel my subscription?

The process for cancelling your subscription may vary depending on the service or product you have subscribed to. Typically, you can cancel your subscription through your account settings or by contacting customer support. It is important to check the terms and conditions of your subscription to see if there are any specific requirements for cancelling or if there are any fees associated with cancelling.

Can I pay via an Invoice?

It depends on the payment options provided by the service or product you are using. Some services or products allow payments via an invoice, while others require payment by credit card or other electronic methods. It's best to check with the specific service or product to see what payment options are available.
